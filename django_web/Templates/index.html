Stress at School

According to the CDC, among children aged 12-17, 36.7% reported having feelings of persistent sadness or hopelessness. These feelings of depression and anxiety are actually increasing over time. In 2003 among children aged 6-17, 5.4% were reported to have been diagnosed with either anxiety or depression. In 2012 that percantage has increased to 8.4%. With these increases in mental illness among children it is important for us to take steps to help identify these feelings early on so that more children can get the proper treatment.
